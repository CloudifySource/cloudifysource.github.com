Come meet us at OpenStack Booth D21 - there's lots going on.

We have officially landed in lovely Boston to a beautiful day, and are getting excited about packed OpenStack starting tomorrow.

Where to find us and what to do...

Cloudify is excited to be able to be a part of the excellent Open Source Day initiative, and will be having our very own track located at the Sheraton Boston Hotel - 2nd Floor - Back Bay Ballroom B.

There are a lot of exciting talks on the agenda - Cloudify Architecture, Apache ARIA, Introducing ONAP, Open vCPE and more.  Join us and be sure to pick up a swag pack.

Talks and other Blah Blah...

We have two excellent talks and a demo theater session you won't want to miss.

  • Join DeWayne Filppi at the Marketplace Demo Theater presenting: Cloudify - Hybrid Orchestration: Automation Across and Between Heterogenous Platforms, Monday, May 8, 1:55PM
  • Join Arthur Berezin for his talk How Cloud Native VNFs on OpenStack will Change the Telecom Industry, Hynes Convention Center - Level Three - MR 312, Tuesday May 9, 11:15AM
  • Join me (Sharone Zitzman) for an excellent panel discussion on the Amazon Effect on Open Source Cloud Business Models, Hynes Convention Center - Level Three - MR 312, Wednesday May 10, 3:30PM
  • There will also be a great talk about Cloudify given by Cissy Liu of H3C on Realize the Ability of Application Orchestration in OpenStack With Cloudify Hynes Convention Center - Level Two - MR 206, Monday May 8, 3:55PM.
