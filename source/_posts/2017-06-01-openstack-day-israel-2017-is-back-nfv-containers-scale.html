OpenStack Day Israel has once again made its way back to Tel Aviv! On June 5th, over 350 developer, IT Ops, and system architect professionals will be joining an elite group of Israeli and international OpenStack superstars who are going to be speaking about all different facets of OpenStack in the real world. Prepare yourself for outstanding talks on Kubernetes, OpenDaylight, High Availability, Capacity Managment, Ceph, and much more. We have two great OpenStack customer stories from Israeli companies Kenshoo and TeraSky as well as a fantastic K8s panel moderated by the one and only Lisa-Marie Namphy.

The venue, once again, this year is the Tel Aviv Fairgrounds. Join us for great presentations, delicious food, t-shirts, and much more!. You can register here.

Our phenomenal lineup of keynotes includes:

  • Majors Eyal David and Shahaf Marom from the Israeli Defense Forces - will be speaking about how they brought OpenStack into the Israeli army and they built their teams to work with DevOps best practices.
  • Christian Carrasco of OnBrella - will be speaking about the origins of the Cloud and how OpenStack was the natural evolution of the internet.
  • Zeev Likwornik and Tal Barenboim of Amdocs - will be speaking about lessons they have learned from the cloud adoption of global telecom operators.

More of our powerhouse speaker list includes:
