Hot on the heels of Mobile World Congress, Team Cloudify is ready to rock the Santa Clara Convention Center for the Open Networking Summit! We are proud sponsors of ONS this year and we are excited to be displaying more of Cloudify's NFV orchestration prowess to the NFV & SDN world.

Our theme for this event is Pure-Play, Open Source, Open Standards, Multi-VIM NFV Orchestration. Coudify has definitely proven its capabilites already at MWC with our demos in collaboration with Metaswitch and Intel. We will be showing this and more and at the upcoming event at our booth SU14.

We also have a number of talks and events to share as well, and we hope to see you join us.

Our Events

Presentations

Sivan Barzily will be speaking at the event in two back-to-back presentations on TOSCA. As one of the chairs of the Instance Model Group of the TOSCA TC, Sivan will be speaking about what TOSCA means for cloud orchestration in general and telecom NFV in particular.

You can see more about the talks taking place on Wednesday, March 16th from 2pm PT here and here.

Meetup

We are proud to be holding our inaugural event for a new meetup group in the Bay Area that we have started on Open Networking, NFV and other Open Source Cloud initiatives. Sivan will also be giving the first talk entitled: "Fully Open Multi-VIM NFV Orchestration With Demo" where she will speak about using open source tools and open architecture principles to automate a multi-cloud NFV environment.

The event will take place March 14 at 6:30pm PT in the Hacker Dojo: 599 Fairchild Dr, Mountain View, CA 94043.

Podcast

The OpenStack & Beyond podcast is also returning with another not-to-be-missed live audiocast directly from the Open Networking Summit to take place on March 17th at 8am PT.

The event will include a variety of telecom and OpenStack experts and will be hosted by our own Nati Shalom and Arthur Berezin.

Catch the live Google Hangout and feel free to join in the conversation by tweeting to @openstackil or using the hashtag #OStackast.
