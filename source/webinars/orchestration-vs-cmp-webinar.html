Why Orchestration is the future for Cloud Management Platforms

In this webinar we will highlight the up and coming role of Orchestration and why its leaving Cloud Management Platforms behind. Orchestration has been around for a few years now, but it has recently been discovered and put to use by more and more IT Operations Managers, trying to automate their production workloads, and save both time and money.

In the new 4.0 Cloudify release, Cloud Management is taking an even greater role, as self services portals, enhanced security and model driven design are being greatly enhanced.
