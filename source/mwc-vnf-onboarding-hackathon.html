Virtual Network Function (VNF) Onboarding Hackathon

Register to join!

Calling all NFV industry professionals to come together and accelerate NFV deployments

There is still space in the first Virtual Network Function (VNF) Onboarding Hackathon that will take place at Mobile World Congress 2017 at the VMware stand, Hall 3, Stand K10. The Hackathon aims to accelerate cloud-based telecom service deployments by addressing key challenges of Network Functions Virtualization (NFV).

This event is not just for developers; CTOs, Director of Sales Engineering, Engineering Managers, Technical Product Managers, Sales Engineers, etc. will benefit from this learning opportunity.

Learn How:

  • TOSCA blueprints add value to your product offerings
  • To explain and articulate NFV/VNF value to your customers, and lead design and architecture discussions regarding deployment
  • Industry trends are shaping the NFV landscape and your customer’s pain points and challenges in deployment
  • To create and modify TOSCA blueprints for your customer deployments
  • To make VNF on boarding easier for your customers

You can read more about the Hackathon here: https://blogs.vmware.com/telco

Agenda at a glance

Wednesday March 1st, Hall 3 Stand K10, VMware booth.

  • 2:30pm – 3:00pm Theater Session – overview of the initiative.
  • 3:00pm – 5:30pm VNF Onboarding Session
  • 5:30pm – 6:30pm VNF Hackathon Reception
