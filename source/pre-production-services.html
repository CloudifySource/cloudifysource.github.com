Cloudify Pre-Production Services

Properly Prepare Your Enterprise Application to Use Cloudify Cloud Orchestration

Cloudify is now launching pre-production services specifically to help your organization use Cloudify Cloud Orchestration in your Technology Stack. Saving you time, money, and most importantly, lots of headaches.

Cloudify Pre-Production Services makes it even easier for enterprises to start on their way to autonomous application management in the cloud. With increased early assessment, training and deployment assistance, you can cut your deployment time by over 30% compared to unassisted deployment.

Service Benefits:

  • Complete On-Boarding; from initial analysis to working developmental environment
  • In Depth Assessment of Client's Current Stack
  • Developed Prototype
  • Cloudify Training
  • Supporting Deployment Activities, including Custom Blueprints, Testing, and Integration
