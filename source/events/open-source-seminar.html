Israel Open Source Seminar

Exclusive Event

March 19, 2017

09:00 a.m. - 1:00 p.m. Israel


Marc Cohn, the Executive Director, OPEN-O and Vice President, Network Strategy at the The Linux Foundation, will be headlining this half day seminar of the role of Open Source in the ongoing Network Virtualization Function revolution.

Join us to learn and discuss:

  • Openness and open source for telecommunications
  • Knowledge transfer between the ECOMP and OPEN-O communities
  • Learn how open source is reshaping standards
  • Examine the evolving open source methodology
  • Learn more about ONAP including why the merge makes sense
  • Get an overview of OPEN-O

 

On the Agenda:


9:00  Breakfast & Mingling

9:20  Sessions and Panels

12:30pm - 1:30pm  Lunch


Marc Cohn, Vice President Network Strategy at The Linux Foundation, has a proven track record as a senior executive in networking and telecommunications software, systems, and services businesses. He has held senior strategy, marketing, product management, and strategic alliances leadership roles, in startups, mid-size, and Multi-National Corporations.
